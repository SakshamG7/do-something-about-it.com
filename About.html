A Message to All: Share This Website and Raise Awareness!

This website was originally created by me, Saksham Goel, in Grade 8 (Updated content and citations in 2022) as part of a school project focused on environmental issues. I chose to address e-waste and climate change because of my passion for technology and my commitment to recycling and reusing devices. Climate change is a serious global problem, and these two topics are deeply interconnected.

In keeping with the theme of sustainability, this website is hosted on recycled hardware. The server is an old Raspberry Pi that was collecting dust, and the device hosting the website is a broken laptop I saved from being discarded. Together, they demonstrate the potential of reusing technology to make a positive impact.

For more about my current work, you can visit my business website: AceIQ, where I help businesses transition to the digital world.

This website is a verified and official source of information, created to raise awareness about climate change and e-waste.

How to Cite This Website

If you would like to cite this website in your research or project, use one of the following formats:

MLA 8 Format:

Goel, Saksham. Let's Do Something About It. Do Something About It, 2020, https://www.do-something-about-it.com. Accessed Day Month Year.

In-text citation (MLA 8): (Goel)

APA Format (7th Edition):

Goel, S. (2020). Let's Do Something About It. Do Something About It. https://www.do-something-about-it.com

In-text citation (APA): (Goel, 2020)

Chicago Format (Bibliography):

Goel, Saksham. 2020. Let's Do Something About It. https://www.do-something-about-it.com.

Chicago Format (Footnote):
Saksham Goel, Let's Do Something About It, 2020, https://www.do-something-about-it.com.

In-text citation (Chicago Author-Date): (Goel 2020)

Harvard Format:

Goel, S., 2020. Let's Do Something About It. Do Something About It. Available at: https://www.do-something-about-it.com [Accessed Day Month Year].

In-text citation (Harvard): (Goel, 2020)
